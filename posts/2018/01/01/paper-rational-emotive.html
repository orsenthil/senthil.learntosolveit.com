Brief Introduction to Rational Emotive Behaviour Therapy. by W Froggatt gives an overview of the Rational Emotive Behavior Therapy, a Cognitive Behavior Therapy school of though in psychology.

The topic itself is accessible and is easy to read. The paper has suggestion on number of books that cover this topic for further reading and study.
