The [Zebra](https://help.gnome.org/users/aisleriot/stable/Zebra.html.en) variant of AisleRiot game is played with two decks. The top row has the two decks to arrange. The player has to arrange the cards with opposite colors. Like Black and Red go alternate on top of each. The bottom is same as solitaire, but in the zebra variant, the top row is arranged alternately.
