I like the [linkding](https://linkding.link) link manager. I realized the extension is provided only for Chrome and Firefox, as publishing for Safari and Mac will require the developer to pay yearly developer fees. The best way is to build one yourself. Use this [orsenthil/safari-linkding-extension](https://github.com/orsenthil/safari-linkding-extension) to build the linkding extension for your safari on mac.
