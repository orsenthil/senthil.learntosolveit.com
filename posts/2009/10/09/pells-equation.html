Pell's Equation

Senthil Kumaran

2009-10-09 20:56

Comments

Source

x^2 - n y^2 = 1

(Pell's equation) which is named after the English mathematician John Pell. It was studied by Brahmagupta in the 7th century, as well as by Fermat in the 17th century.
